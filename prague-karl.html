Karlštejn Castle & Pilsen Tour

Experience the magic of medieval Czech history and world-famous beer in one unforgettable day

Tour Highlights

Embark on a journey through time as you explore the magnificent Karlštejn Castle, a 14th-century Gothic masterpiece built by Charles IV. Marvel at the stunning architecture, royal chambers, and the famous Chapel of the Holy Cross. Then, visit the birthplace of Pilsner beer in Pilsen, where you'll tour the historic brewery and enjoy a refreshing tasting session.

8-hour guided tour
Skip-the-line castle entry
Beer tasting in Pilsen
Private transportation
